Finding Your Group

Your load shedding group (also called feeder group) determines which hours your area loses power.

How to find it:

  1. Visit your utility website (LESCO, IESCO, K-Electric, etc.)
  2. Look for "Load Shedding Schedule" or "Feeder Schedule"
  3. Search your area name / address
  4. Your group number (1–8) will be listed

Don't know it? Start with Group 4 — it's the most common. You can change it anytime in Settings.
